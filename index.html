WHY SINGAPORE?

Singapore: Cultural and culinary melting pot

Singapore, officially the Republic of Singapore, is an island country and city-state in Southeast Asia. Located at the southern tip of the Malay Peninsula, about 85 mi (137 km) north of the Equator, Singapore is known for its tropical climate, ethnic diversity, innovative architecture, and maritime history.

In recognition of its multi-ethnic and multi-cultural population, Singapore has four official languages: English, Malay, Mandarin, and Tamil. Multi-racialism is enshrined in the constitution and continues to shape national policies in education, housing, and politics. (Wikipedia)
