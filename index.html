WHY SINGAPORE?

Singapore: Cultural and culinary melting pot

Singapore, officially the Republic of Singapore, is an island country and city-state in Southeast Asia. Located at the southern tip of the Malay Peninsula, about 85 miles (137 kilometres) north of the Equator, Singapore is known for its tropical climate, ethnic diversity, innovative architecture, and maritime history.
